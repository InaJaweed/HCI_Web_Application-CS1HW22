FAQ

How do I place an order?

To place an order, simply add the items you wish to purchase to your shopping cart and follow the checkout process.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards, as well as PayPal and Apple Pay.

Can I return or exchange an item?

Yes, we have a 30-day return policy for all items purchased on our website. If you have any troubles please see our "contact us" page

How long will it take to receive my order?

Delivery times vary depending on your location and the shipping method you choose at checkout. Standard delivery within the UK typically takes 3-5 business days.

How do I check the status of my order?

You can check the status of your order by logging into your account and viewing your order history. You will also receive email updates as your order is processed and shipped.
